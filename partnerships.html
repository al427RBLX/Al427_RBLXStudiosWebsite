Taiwan Informal Allies Committee

Roblox Group | Partnership with Al427_RBLX Studios

The Informal Allies Committee is a fictional committee of the Republic of China (Taiwan) created specifically for ROBLOX. The objective of this committee is to accept ally requests from any groups, disregarding their purpose or category, but through an implicit or informal alliance towards the main group to encourage communication and unity, since the Ministry of Foreign Affairs can't accept alliances from any groups, unless it is for diplomatic relations (RO-nations, NGOs, intergovermental organizations, etc.) that are mainly for political purposes and/or based from real-life.
